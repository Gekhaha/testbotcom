Phase 1: Initial Development and Prototyping

The first phase focuses on laying the foundation of the project. This includes brainstorming the concept, creating the initial design, and setting up basic infrastructure. We will also begin prototyping the core features and test them for functionality. During this phase, we focus on:

  • Defining project goals and objectives
  • Initial design and planning
  • Creating a basic prototype
  • Testing core features for feasibility

Phase 2: Development and Refinement

In the second phase, the project moves into active development. This is where features and functionalities are implemented, and the product begins to take shape. The primary goal is to refine the initial prototype by adding enhancements, improving performance, and addressing any technical issues. Key activities during this phase include:

  • Building out core features and functionality
  • Refining design and UI/UX
  • Optimizing performance and fixing bugs
  • Collecting feedback and iterating on design
